# How to Create Your Chamber Partnership Explainer Video with Pictory

## Step 1: Sign Up for Pictory

1. Go to https://pictory.ai
2. Click "Start Free Trial" or "Get Started"
3. Choose the Standard plan ($23/month) which includes HD video export
4. Complete the signup process

## Step 2: Create Your Video

1. Click "Script to Video" on the dashboard
2. Paste the content from `CHAMBER_TOURISM_PARTNERSHIP.md` into the script box
3. Click "Proceed"

## Step 3: Customize Your Video

Pictory will automatically:
- Select relevant stock footage for each section
- Generate a professional voiceover
- Add text overlays for key points
- Create transitions between scenes

You can customize:
- **Voiceover**: Choose a professional male or female voice
- **Music**: Add background music (choose something corporate and uplifting)
- **Branding**: Add your logo to the video
- **Colors**: Match your brand colors

## Step 4: Review and Export

1. Preview the video
2. Make any final adjustments
3. Click "Download" and select HD quality
4. Your video will be ready in 5-10 minutes

## Recommended Settings

- **Video Length**: 2-3 minutes (Pictory will automatically pace the content)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (landscape for professional presentations)
- **Voiceover**: Professional female voice (sounds more approachable for Chamber audiences)
- **Music**: Corporate/Uplifting (low volume so it doesn't overpower the voiceover)
- **Text Style**: Clean, modern fonts with your brand colors

## Tips for Best Results

- The partnership document is already written in a video-friendly format
- Pictory will automatically highlight key statistics like "50% discount" and "15% revenue giveback"
- The ROI table will be displayed as text overlays with relevant business footage
- Total time from upload to finished video: 10-15 minutes
