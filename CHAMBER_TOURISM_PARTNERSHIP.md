# ScrapeX Partnership Program for Chambers of Commerce & Tourism Boards

## A Community Support System for Local Business Growth

ScrapeX is a community support system designed to strengthen local business ecosystems. We partner with Chambers of Commerce and Tourism Boards to provide their members with a powerful tool that finds new customers, handles incoming calls, and drives revenue growth. Our partnership model is designed to be a win-win-win: a win for your organization, a win for your members, and a win for the local community.

## What the ScrapeX Platform Does for Your Members

ScrapeX is an all-in-one platform that helps businesses find new customers and manage incoming calls. Hereâ€™s what it does:

### 1. Find New Customers

ScrapeX can scrape any business directory, including your own Chamber or Tourism board member list, to find potential customers. It extracts detailed information, including:

- Business Name
- Phone Number
- Website
- Address
- Owner/Decision-Maker Contact Information (Name, Email, LinkedIn)

### 2. Automated Outreach

Our multi-lingual voice service can automatically call these prospects, introduce your member's business, and schedule appointments. The service sounds human and conversational, and can speak fluently in English, Spanish, French, Mandarin, Portuguese, and German.

### 3. 24/7 Call Answering

The voice service can also answer incoming calls 24/7, ensuring that your members never miss a lead. It can:

- Answer common questions
- Capture leads and inquiries
- Route calls to the appropriate person
- Schedule appointments
- Provide business referrals
