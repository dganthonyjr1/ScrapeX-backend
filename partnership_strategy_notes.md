### Target Organizations
- Chambers of Commerce

### Core Offer to Chambers/Tourism Boards

**Services Provided:**
- AI Virtual Assistant / Voice Agent
- Missed-call handling
- Lead capture
- Visitor info
- Booking assistance
- FAQ support
- Business referrals

**What They Get:**
- 50% discounted service
- 15% revenue contribution back to the organization
- Community reinvestment (events, scholarships, marketing, etc.)

### Positioning
- NOT selling software
- Offering a **community support system**

### Pitch Structure

**Short Version:**
"We help tourism organizations and chambers support their members by providing AI-powered virtual assistants that handle calls, answer questions, capture leads, and improve visitor experience. We offer this at a 50% reduced rate and give 15% of any resulting revenue back to the organization to support community initiatives."

**Expanded Version:**
"We provide AI-powered Virtual Assistants that answer calls, handle FAQs, capture leads, route inquiries, and support visitors and local businesses 24/7.

Our partnership model gives tourism boards and chambers a 50% reduced service rate, and we return 15% of any revenue generated back to the organization to fund events, scholarships, destination marketing, or workforce development.

The goal is to strengthen the local business ecosystem while improving how visitors and residents engage with the destination."

### Service Agreement Structure
- 50% discounted monthly rate
- No long-term lock-in (start with 90-day pilot)
- 15% of applicable revenue returned to organization
- Funds allocated at organization's discretion
- Organization allows SIA to present services
- Dedicated referral link provided
- Educational session opportunity
- Initial 90-day pilot
- Option to renew
- 30-day written notice for termination

## Source: CommunityPartnershipProposal.pdf

### Overview
Sudden Impact Agency provides virtual assistants designed to support chambers of commerce, tourism boards, and destination organizations by improving visitor engagement, reducing staff workload, and supporting local businesses through automated call handling and inquiry management.

### What the Virtual Assistant Does
- Answers incoming calls 24/7
- Handles common visitor and business questions
- Captures leads and inquiries
- Routes calls and requests appropriately
- Supports local business referrals
- Reduces missed calls and staff overload

### Who It Helps
- Visitors and tourists
- Chamber and tourism staff
- Local businesses and members
- Event organizers and community programs

### Partnership Model
- 50% reduced service rate for chamber/tourism initiatives
- 15% revenue contribution returned to the organization
- Funds may be used for scholarships, events, destination marketing, workforce development, or community programs

### Additional Benefits
- Educational presentation for members or stakeholders
- Dedicated referral link for the organization
- Priority support and onboarding
- Month to month (no long term commitment)

### Next Steps
Schedule a 15 minute walkthrough to explore how this program can support your organization and community.

## Integration Requirements for ScrapeX

### Chamber/Tourism Partnership Features to Build:

1. **Partnership Pricing Tier**
   - 50% discount for Chamber/Tourism organizations
   - Automatic 15% revenue sharing calculation
   - Monthly billing with no long term contracts

2. **Revenue Sharing Dashboard**
   - Track revenue generated from Chamber member referrals
   - Calculate 15% contribution automatically
   - Generate monthly reports for Chamber showing community impact
   - Allow Chamber to designate fund allocation (scholarships, events, marketing)

3. **Referral System**
   - Generate unique referral links for each Chamber/Tourism board
   - Track which businesses signed up through Chamber referral
   - Attribute revenue to correct Chamber for revenue sharing

4. **Educational Resources**
   - Create presentation materials for Chamber member meetings
   - Provide educational sessions on how the system works
   - Offer training for Chamber staff

5. **Member Benefits Portal**
   - Chamber members get priority access
   - Simplified onboarding for Chamber referred businesses
   - Dedicated support channel for Chamber partnerships

6. **Community Impact Reporting**
   - Monthly reports showing:
     - Number of calls handled
     - Leads captured for local businesses
     - Revenue generated for community fund
     - Visitor satisfaction metrics
